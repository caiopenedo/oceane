    the ocean is inspiring


    The ocean is at the center of who we are. Even if you’ve never seen the ocean, you’re touched by it every single day. It produces half the air you and us breathe every day, the food we eat and the water we drink.

    You can’t help but be inspired by the ocean. From majestic whales to jaw-dropping waves, colorful coral reefs to graceful sea turtles, the ocean can capture your heart and soul like nothing else on earth.

    This is what we’re fighting to protect. Because people and communities depend on the ocean every single day. I hope you’ll be just as inspired as we are to join us in that effort.

    it’s our ocean


    One of the things I’m most proud of at Oceane© is that we tackle the greatest global challenges facing our ocean today.

    Even more, we focus on long-term solutions that promote a healthy ocean, abundant wildlife and thriving coastal communities.

    What happens to the ocean impacts all of us. The ocean is our responsibility, but we can’t do it alone—we need to join forces with you and people around the world to keep our ocean and our coastal communities healthy and prosperous.
